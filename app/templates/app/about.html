About Us


A French company, one of the largest European DIY retailers. Founded in 1923, it is part of the Adeo group, which belongs to the "Mulier Family Association".



Mission


The mission of Leroy Merlin is to ensure economic growth and solve home improvement and improve the quality of life of customers through the production and sale of building materials and household goods that meet the requirements for high quality and competitiveness, low prices for goods. This should allow Leroy Merlin's shareholders and/or partners to receive optimal profits, and its staff to receive decent and fair remuneration.
